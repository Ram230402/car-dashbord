#  Car Sales & Performance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Car Sales & Performance Analytics Dashboard** developed using Power BI. It provides insights into sales trends, customer preferences, and product performance across different regions.
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand key metrics and make **data-driven business decisions** in the automotive domain.
## Objectives
* Analyze car sales performance across regions
* Identify top-performing models and categories
* Understand customer preferences and trends
* Monitor revenue, sales growth, and distribution
## Tech Stack
* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* DAX (KPIs & Calculations)
* SQL (Data processing - optional)
* CSV / Dataset (Car Sales Data)
## Key Features
* Interactive KPI cards (Total Sales, Revenue, Units Sold)
* Sales analysis by region and category
* Top-performing car models visualization
* Dynamic filters for real-time insights
* Clean and modern dashboard design
##  Key Insights
* Certain regions contribute the highest revenue share
* Top car models dominate overall sales
* Seasonal trends impact sales performance
* Customer preferences vary across regions
## Business Impact
* Helps identify top-performing products
* Supports sales strategy optimization
* Enables better inventory and planning decisions
* Improves understanding of customer demand
